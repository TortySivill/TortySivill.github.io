Research



Passionate PhD researcher specialising in explainable artificial intelligence with over 8 years of computer science academic experience. I am detail-oriented and an excellent academic writer with multiple publications at top tier machine learning venues (AISTATS, Frontiers) and an award-winning, published, undergraduate dissertation. I am a confident public speaker and have delivered invited talks at public events, industry workshops and academic venues. I was invited to facilitate at one of the prestigious Turing Data Study Groups resulting in a published report. I am a strong communicator and have extensive experience leading seminars and lab workshops for university students. I have co-hosted the Turing Institute’s podcast and was selected as a panelist for the AI UK Conference.
